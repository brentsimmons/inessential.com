@dbid 2398 @title NetNewsWire Dock Menu Tip @pubDate Fri Feb 14 13:31:19 -0800 2003 @modDate Fri Feb 14 13:31:19 -0800 2003 If you click-and-hold, ctrl-click, or right-click (if you have a two-button mouse) on NetNewsWire’s icon in the Dock, the menu displays unread messages.

You probably knew that already.

But—you may not have known that if you hold down the option key, then, instead of opening the headline in the browser, you go to that headline in NetNewsWire.
