@dbid 2400 @title NetNewsWire easy subscribing @pubDate Sat Feb 15 11:38:50 -0800 2003 @modDate Sat Feb 15 11:38:50 -0800 2003 Another of my favorite NetNewsWire features is how you can subscribe using the Services menu.

With any app—Safari, Chimera, etc.—that supports Services, all you have to do is:

1. Select a URL. (As in the address field in Safari.)

2. Choose Subscribe in NetNewsWire from the Services menu.
