@dbid 2612 @title NetNewsWire 1.0.4fc1 @pubDate Sat Aug 23 17:24:35 -0700 2003 @modDate Sat Aug 23 17:26:26 -0700 2003 NetNewsWire 1.0.4fc1 is up.

There are just a few small changes since the previous beta: turned off underlined text when showing HTML differences, turned off tooltips for headlines in the Combined View, and updated the Help book.

This is a final candidate release, which means we’re looking for major bugs, bugs that have to be fixed before shipping 1.0.4.
