@dbid 2601 @title Scroll-wheel-click in Safari @pubDate Fri Aug 15 23:34:01 -0700 2003 @modDate Fri Aug 15 23:34:01 -0700 2003 Here’s another I thing discovered in Safari. (Again, maybe it’s old news, but maybe not.)

If you have a mouse with a scroll wheel, then if you scroll-wheel-click on a link (or bookmark in the Bookmarks Bar), then the link will open in a new tab.

This is quicker than ctrl-clicking (or right-clicking) and choosing Open Link in New Tab.

(It probably requires that you have tabbed browsing turned on, of course.)
