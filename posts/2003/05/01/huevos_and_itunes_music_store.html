@dbid 2477 @title Huevos and iTunes Music Store @pubDate Thu May 01 17:46:28 -0700 2003 @modDate Thu May 01 17:47:06 -0700 2003 Spencer Lynn sent me email with a tip: you can configure Huevos to kick off a search in the iTunes Music Store.

In Huevos’ Preferences, create a new search engine with a name like iTunes Music Store. The URL should be itms://phobos.apple.com/WebObjects/MZSearch.woa/wa/search?term=%@

Give it a keyboard shortcut if you want, and click OK.
