@dbid 2526 @title Brent’s Law of Wikis @pubDate Tue Jun 03 17:20:06 -0700 2003 @modDate Tue Jun 03 17:20:26 -0700 2003 I want to like wikis. But for some reason every wiki I’ve ever seen is, well, ugly.

So I propose Brent’s Law of Wikis: the set of people who use wikis and the set of people who know how to make websites look good are mutually exclusive.

Feel free to prove me wrong!
