@dbid 2688 @title Aaron Hillegass revisits NSController @pubDate Tue Nov 04 12:55:04 -0800 2003 @modDate Tue Nov 04 12:55:04 -0800 2003 Aaron Hillegass had previously been quite critical of the new NSController layer in Panther; he revisits NSController in a new post to the cocoa-dev mailing list.

Aaron writes: “After many demonstrations of the power of NSController (‘Aaron, this app was 252,500 lines of code, but with NSController it is only 3!’), I must agree with them: life is better. They also agreed with me: there is much room for improvement.”
