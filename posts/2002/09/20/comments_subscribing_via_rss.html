@dbid 2141 @title Comments Subscribing via RSS @pubDate Fri Sep 20 10:45:50 -0700 2002 @modDate Fri Sep 20 10:45:50 -0700 2002 At a suggestion from Dave Winer, instead of adding email subscriptions for comments pages, I added RSS subscriptions.

On every comments page (on this site and on ranchero.com), near the bottom of each page, there’s a link to an RSS feed for just that page. That way you can keep up with any conversations you may be interested in.

I might also add email subscriptions, too—but I think RSS solves the problem very nicely.
