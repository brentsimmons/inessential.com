@dbid 129 @title Queue Suite Updated @link http://queue.macrobyte.net/ @pubDate Mon Apr 08 10:13:59 -0700 2002 @modDate Mon Apr 08 10:13:59 -0700 2002 Macrobyte Resources: “The Queue Suite is a queue processing daemon for Frontier and Radio. A queue daemon processes items one at a time, in the order in which they are received. This is also known as a First In First Out Processing System. The Queue Suite is used in Conversant, the AttSearchEngine, RCSearch, and various other tools and software produced by Macrobyte.”
