@dbid 2231 @title NetNewsWire Lite 1.0.2b2 @link http://ranchero.com/software/netnewswire/beta.php @pubDate Tue Oct 22 09:01:13 -0700 2002 @modDate Tue Oct 22 09:01:13 -0700 2002 NetNewsWire Lite 1.0.2b2 helps reduce bandwidth consumption.

Along with supporting ETag and Last-Modified headers, it also resets the refresh clock when you do a manual refresh-all. The default subscriptions now includes only large sites—no weblogs—so as not to cause folks to pay extra charges for bandwidth used by their sites.

Other changes are listed on the change notes page.
